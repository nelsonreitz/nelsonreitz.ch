Who am I?

I am a Swiss Front-End Designer, I build user interfaces for websites and apps. Currently based in Lausanne working at DevFactory agency.

I graduated in Visual Communication in Geneva University of Art and Design (HEAD). Since then, I have been working as a web developer and designer. In my free time, I enjoy long walks, drawing, playing music and good beer. ✌️

My work

Coming from a design background, I am moving more and more towards the role of a Front-end developer as I find it more fun to actually build things. Being between these two worlds of design and development hugely helps working with a team as I can usually relate to both roles.
